# Renewable Energy Certificates

A blockchain-based system for issuing, tracking, and trading renewable energy certificates (RECs) to incentivize clean energy production on the Stacks blockchain.

## Overview
This project implements a comprehensive renewable energy certificate system that enables transparent tracking of renewable energy production and creates a marketplace for carbon credits and energy certificates.

## Features
- **Certificate Issuance**: Automated REC generation from verified energy production
- **Energy Verification**: Multi-source validation of renewable energy output
- **Trading Marketplace**: Peer-to-peer trading of energy certificates
- **Carbon Credits**: Environmental impact tracking and credit generation
- **Producer Management**: Registration and verification of renewable energy producers

## Smart Contracts
### energy-certificates
The core contract managing renewable energy certificate lifecycle, producer verification, certificate trading, and carbon credit accounting.

## Use Cases
- Solar and wind energy certificate management
- Carbon offset trading and verification
- Corporate renewable energy procurement
- Environmental impact reporting and compliance
